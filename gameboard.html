Game instructions

Goal

In this game, two players will take on the role of a character from the Game of Thrones universe and race each other in a battle to capture the throne in King's Landing. In order to successfully challenge the capital you need to have an army big enough to defeat Queen Cersei Lannister's army. If you are the first player to show up in King's landing with an army of at least 100 000 soldiers, you are the true winner!

Board spaces

Space Description Action
Start You receive 10 000 soldiers.
Regular space Each of the five main zones on the gameboard has four regular spaces. You will receive 5000 soldiers.
Battle space In each of the five zones on the gameboard there is a battle space. When you land on one, you will draw a battle card. You may lose soldiers, but you may also get lucky and gain soldiers.
Dragon's Den You better not land on this space. There are dragons here, and they will cost you 10 000 soldiers.
The capital This is the goal of your journey, but you must come here with at least 100 000 soldiers to win the game. If you don't have enough soldiers, nothing will happen here.

How to win

The first player that reaches the capital with at least 100 000 soldiers has won the game.
